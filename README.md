### 1. [Telco Customer Churn Prediction](PASTE_YOUR_TELCO_REPO_URL_HERE)
* **Objective:** Predict whether a subscriber is likely to cancel their telecom subscription based on historical consumer behavioral patterns.
* **Algorithms Benchmarked:** Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost
* **Highlights:** End-to-end feature preprocessing (StandardScaler & OneHotEncoder), feature importance mapping, and optimization of an XGBoost champion model to minimize corporate revenue loss.

### 2. [Employee Attrition Analysis & Prediction](PASTE_YOUR_ATTRITION_REPO_URL_HERE)
* **Objective:** Analyze workplace and demographic metrics to predict individual employee turnover risk and isolate key operational burnout drivers.
* **The Challenge:** Faced a severe class imbalance problem typical of corporate HR data, causing standard classifiers to overlook employees at risk of quitting.
* **The Solution:** Implemented **SMOTE (Synthetic Minority Over-sampling Technique)** to balance the dataset, boosting minority class recall by nearly **300%** while establishing a resilient **82% base evaluation accuracy**.
